# Agentic-Reseller-Success-Coach

An intelligent **AI-powered agent** designed to coach and empower resellers, e-commerce entrepreneurs, and affiliate marketers to achieve consistent success.

Built with agentic AI workflows to provide real-time guidance, strategy optimization, performance tracking, and personalized coaching.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Agentic AI Coaching**: Autonomous AI agents that analyze your reselling performance and deliver contextual advice
- **Strategy Optimizer**: Smart recommendations for product selection, pricing, marketing, and scaling
- **Performance Dashboard**: Real-time metrics and insights on sales, profits, and growth opportunities
- **Personalized Learning Paths**: Adaptive coaching based on your current skill level and business stage
- **Market Intelligence**: Automated competitor analysis and trending product discovery
- **Actionable Workflows**: Step-by-step playbooks and automation suggestions
